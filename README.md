## Community Elderly Health Information Management System

> [Click me download source code / Try it online](https://www.devquizdone.online/detail/3bb1dfcc1a21441c9b20a31cf856ed99/ghb20250917) 

### 1. Related Technologies
- Backend: `Java`, `JavaWeb` / `Springboot`.
- Frontend: `Vue`, `HTML` / `CSS` / `Javascript`, etc.
- Database: `MySQL`

### 2. Related Software (Any one of the listed software can be used to run)
- `IDEA`
- `Eclipse`
- `Visual Studio Code(VScode)`
- `Navicat`
- etc.

### 3. Function Description
The system is divided into (roles): regular users, administrators.

**Regular User Functions:**
1. Login
2. Register
3. Homepage
4. Forum
5. Announcements
6. Medical Insurance Information
7. Personal Center
8. Medical History
9. Health Information
10. Medication Usage
11. Appointments



**Doctor Functions:**
1. Login
2. Register
3. Change Password
4. Personal Information
5. Appointment Management
6. Medical History Management
7. Health Information Management
8. Medication Management
9. Medication Stock Management
10. Medication Usage Management
11. Medical Insurance Information Management
12. Forum Management
13. Announcement Management
14. Carousel Image Management



**Administrator Functions:**
1. Login
2. Personal Center (change password, personal information)
3. Administrator Management
4. Doctor Management
5. User Management
6. Appointment Management
7. Medical History Management
8. Health Information Management
9. Medication Management
10. Medication Stock Management
11. Medication Usage Management
12. Medical Insurance Information Management
13. Forum Management
14. Announcement Type Management
15. Health Type Management
16. Medication Type Management
17. Medical Insurance Type Management
18. Carousel Image Management
